Welcome to VPR's Artist Mug Gallery

Our most recent Artist Mug is by Althea Freeman-Miller of Burlington whose studio is in Richmond. Althea has created a charming woodcut showing an early fall scene of a Vermonter tending her clothesline in the fresh breeze that harkens back to a simpler time. Tall sunflowers frame the image.

About Artist Mugs

VPR began commissioning mug designs from Vermont artists in 1992. Since then, more than 50 mugs have been created to celebrate VPR and its community. Browse our mug gallery to see all of our mug designs. Unfortunately, at this time the mugs from past drives highlighted in this gallery are not available for sale.
